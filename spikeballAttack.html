Keep the Jewel safe from the spikeballs



Themes, Options, and the Game



Access many different themes, options, instructions, and play right from the home menu



The Game



Keep the Jewel safe from all of the spikeballs with careful maneuvers. Carefully move through all of the spikeballs. Compare your score with your friends and see who has a better score.



Difficulties



Think this is too easy? Make the difficulty medium, or hard and see what is the best score that you can get. Compare your score with your friends and see who is better.



Themes



Access 4 different themes to access 4 important times in the year. Use whichever theme fits your mood and see how does it work out.

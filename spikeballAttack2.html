Spikeball Attack 2
Keep the Jewel safe from the spikeballs



Themes, Options, and the Game



Access the all new store, options, instructions, and play right from the home menu



The Game



Play the all new side attack mode in which you never know where the spikeballs will come. Keep the Jewel safe from all of the spikeballs with careful maneuvers. Carefully move through all of the spikeballs. Compare your score with your friends and see who has a better score.



Difficulties



Think this is too easy? Make the difficulty medium, or hard and see what is the best score that you can get. Compare your score with your friends and see who is better.



Store



Buy all new spikeballs and jewels in the all new store. Keep playing until you get enough gold and change your look with a new spikeball or a new jewel.

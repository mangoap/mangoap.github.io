About Us

This company was founded by Paran Sonthalia in 2015. We are a company that develops iOS Apps, and we teach programming for non-profit organizations geared towards young programmers. Included in the apps that we build are apps for the city that we are located in.


Apps We Make

We make games and apps geared towards the general crowd, so that anyone can use our apps. We also make apps for the city when they need us to help.


Classes We Teach

We teach classes for many different organizations so that we can influence the younger crowd to get involved with programming. All of these classes are free so that anyone can attend.
